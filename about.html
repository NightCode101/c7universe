What is Cloud 7?

Cloud 7 is an official Filipino P-pop (Philippine Pop) boy group formed and managed by Sparkle GMA Artist Center. Consisting of seven talented members ranging in age from 13 to 19, they made their initial debut on August 20, 2023, and officially launched on May 23, 2025.

Known for their energetic dance-pop music and youthful spirit, Cloud 7 is recognized as the youngest P-pop group under the Sparkle management roster.

Also known as: C7, CLOUD 7
