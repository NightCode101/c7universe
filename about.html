What is Cloud 7?

Cloud 7 is an official Filipino P-pop (Philippine Pop) boy group managed by Sparkle GMA Artist Center. The seven-member group, ranging in age from 13 to 19 years old, debuted on August 20, 2023, and made their official media launch on May 23, 2025. Known for their dance-pop music and youthful energy, Cloud 7 is recognized as the youngest P-pop group under the Sparkle management roster.

Cloud 7 (2024): Fian, Kairo, Lukas, Egypt, PJ, Migz, and Johann
